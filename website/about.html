About Welmac Engineering

Uganda's leader in structural steel and engineering solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Welmac Engineering & Logistics Co. Ltd has established itself as Uganda's dominant player in construction and structural steel industry. We began operations to facilitate the Welmac Group as part of strategic management planning, introducing state-of-the-art computerized equipment and Tekla steel structure 3D software.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative engineering solutions through advanced technology, industrialized building systems, and internationally recognized safety practices.

Our Vision

To position ourselves as East Africa's leading specialist in mechanical, electrical, and structural engineering solutions.

Strategic Location

Based in Hoima District, Western Uganda - heart of Uganda's industrial development
