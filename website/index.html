Our Engineering Process

1 Consultation & Planning

We begin with a detailed consultation to understand your project requirements. Our team develops comprehensive plans using Tekla 3D modeling software to visualize the final structure.

2 Design & Engineering

Our engineering team creates precise designs optimized for manufacturing and installation. We focus on 'design for production' principles to ensure cost-effective solutions.

3 Precision Manufacturing

Using our 2,000-ton/month capacity facilities, we fabricate components with CNC machines integrated with 3D detailing software for millimeter-perfect accuracy.

4 Quality Control & Testing

All components undergo rigorous testing and inspection to meet international standards and client specifications before leaving our facility.

5 Professional Installation

Our experienced site teams ensure safe and precise installation, adhering to strict safety protocols and project timelines.
