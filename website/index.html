Who We Are

Established in 2018, Welmac Engineering is Uganda's dominant player in construction and structural steel industry. We leverage state-of-the-art computerized equipment and Tekla 3D software to deliver innovative solutions with internationally recognized safety standards.

Our adoption of Industrialized Building Systems (IBS) and precast concrete technology ensures superior quality control, reduced timelines, and minimized manpower dependency.
